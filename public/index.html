Hi 👋🏻, my name is Eivind Lindbråten, and I'm a Norwegian freelancer who loves to play around with code and build stuff. Current area of focus are web and iOS projects — but most of all I like to work on fun, creative and weird projects.

Shown below are some of the projects I have built, either self initated or for clients.👇🏻

tenkekassa.no

Humanistisk Ungdom created a anti prize called "Tenkekassa", highlighting the importance of critical thinking. They wanted a website where people could vote for their favorite (or least favorite) candidate — and I created this site. It's built on Node.js, uses Google's reCATCHPA to prevent voter fraud, and uses Google Sheets as a backend.
